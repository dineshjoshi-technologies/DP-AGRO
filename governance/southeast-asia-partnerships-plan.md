# Southeast Asia Partnership Strategy Plan

## Goal
Establish strategic partnerships across Southeast Asia to support international expansion goals and accelerate user adoption of our agricultural intelligence platform by 2027.

## Context Reviewed
- Strategic Planning Governance Framework
- Government Relations Executive Handbook
- CEO_todolist.json (pending: "Initiate international partnership outreach in Southeast Asia")
- COMPANY_OVERVIEW.md

## Constraints & Non-Goals
- Must align with ethical AI guidelines
- Partnerships must meet carbon neutrality requirements
- Cannot exceed Phase 4 budget allocations
- Focus only on Southeast Asia for Phase 1
- Maintain compliance with regional regulations

## Approach
1. Identify priority countries: Thailand, Vietnam, Philippines, Malaysia, Indonesia
2. Evaluate potential partners against strategic criteria
3. Initiate 3-year partnership roadmap
4. Launch pilot programs with 3 key partners

## Work Breakdown
1. Market Analysis & Partner Identification (Chief Government Relations Officer)
   - Deliverables: Market reports for each country with partner shortlist
   - Acceptance: Reports signed off by CEO

2. Evaluation Framework Development (Chief Marketing Officer)
   - Deliverables: Tiered ranking of 10+ potential partners
   - Acceptance: Matrix approved by Executive Committee

3. Negotiation & Agreement Finalization (Chief Legal Officer)
   - Deliverables: 3 signed MoUs with key partners
   - Acceptance: Drafts approved by legal review team

4. Pilot Program Launch (Chief Operations Officer)
   - Deliverables: Operational pilot programs with baseline KPIs
   - Acceptance: Pilots operational with initial metrics established

## Acceptance Criteria
- MOUs signed with 3 potential partners within 6 months
- Pilot programs operational in 3 priority regions within 12 months
- Partnerships accelerate user adoption metrics by 15% within 18 months
- All partnerships meet carbon neutrality and sustainability standards

## Risks & Mitigations
- Regulatory changes: Monitor through legal consultations
- Partner misalignment: Joint governance with clear ethical commitments
- Resource allocation: Phase rollout to align with budget availability

## Deferrals
- Advanced partnership models: Deferred to post-pilot phase
- Regional expansion beyond Southeast Asia: Deferred to Phase 2 planning
