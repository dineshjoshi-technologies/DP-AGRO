# Implementation Plan

## Overview
This document translates the Strategic Plan into actionable tasks, owners, timelines, and resource allocations.

## Quarterly Breakdown

### Q1 2026 - AI Data Pipeline Prototype
- **Task**: Develop core data ingestion and preprocessing pipeline.
- **Key Subtasks**:
  - Integrate IoT sensor feeds from 50 pilot farms.
  - Build data validation module (accuracy ≥95%).
  - Create initial predictive model training scripts.
- **Owner**: Chief Technology Officer (CTO)
- **Budget**: $500k for engineering resources.
- **Milestone**: MVP ready by end of Q1 2026.

### Q2 2026 - Pilot Farm Acquisition
- **Task**: Secure 500 ha of medicinal plant acreage.
- **Key Subtasks**:
  - Identify and negotiate land leases in Karnataka and Himachal.
  - Obtain organic certification for selected plots.
  - Implement precision irrigation and monitoring.
- **Owner**: Chief Agriculture Officer (CAO)
- **Budget**: $2M for land acquisition and setup.
- **Milestone**: 500 ha under cultivation by end of Q2 2026.

### Q3 2026 - GMP Facility Design
- **Task**: Finalize design of scalable GMP manufacturing facility.
- **Key Subtasks**:
  - Complete engineering schematics for clean rooms and bioreactors.
  - Conduct DA (Design Assurance) documentation.
  - Secure regulatory approvals (FSSAI, FDA).
- **Owner**: Chief Manufacturing Officer (CMO)
- **Budget**: $10M for design and initial construction.
- **Milestone**: Approved facility design by end of Q3 2026.

### Q4 2026 - First Product Launch
- **Task**: Release first premium natural product to market.
- **Key Subtasks**:
  - Finalize product formulation and packaging.
  - Execute pilot marketing campaign.
  - Set up distribution channels (direct-to-farmer and retail).
- **Owner**: Chief Marketing Officer (CMO)
- **Budget**: $3M for production and marketing.
- **Milestone**: Launch event and initial sales by end of Q4 2026.

## Cross‑Functional Dependencies
- **AI Platform** requires data from agriculture pilot (Q2).
- **Manufacturing Facility** relies on finalized product design (Q3).
- **Product Launch** depends on regulatory approvals (CAO & CMO).

## Risk Register
| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Data Quality Issues | Medium | High | Implement continuous data validation; partner with farm tech firms. |
| Land Lease Delays | Low | Medium | Engage legal counsel early; diversify site selections. |
| Regulatory Changes | Medium | High | Maintain active dialogue with agencies; allocate budget for compliance monitoring. |
| Supply Chain Constraints | High | Medium | Develop multi‑source sourcing strategy; maintain safety stock.

- **Farmer Net Profit**: ≥15% increase.
- **AI Model Accuracy**: >90%.
- **Carbon Footprint**: ↓25%.
- **Regulatory Audits**: 100% compliance.
