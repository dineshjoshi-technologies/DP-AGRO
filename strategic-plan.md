# Strategic Plan

## Mission Alignment
- Build trusted AI‑powered medicinal agriculture ecosystem.
- Sustainable farming, research, AI, manufacturing.

## Vision Targets (5‑year)
- Deploy AI decision platform to 10,000 farms.
- Launch 3 premium natural product lines.
- Achieve carbon‑negative manufacturing.

## Primary Objectives
1. **AI Platform**: Develop predictive crop models, automate supply‑chain.
2. **Agricultural Expansion**: Secure 5,000 ha of medicinal plant acreage.
3. **Manufacturing**: Build scalable GMP facility.
4. **Research**: Publish 20 peer‑reviewed papers.
5. **Sustainability**: Reduce water usage 30%.

## Key Metrics
- Revenue growth YoY > 20%.
- Farmer net profit increase ≥15%.
- AI model accuracy > 90%.
- Carbon footprint ↓ 25%.
- Regulatory compliance audits passed.

## Governance
- Quarterly reviews with C‑suite.
- Risk register updated monthly.
- Innovation budget 10% of OPEX.
