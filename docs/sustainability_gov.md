# Sustainability Governance Framework

**Purpose**: Establish clear guidelines for sustainable practices across agricultural, manufacturing, and research operations.

**Key Components**:
1. **Environmental Compliance**: Adherence to national/international environmental regulations.
2. **Resource Management**: Tracks water, soil, and energy usage with AI-driven optimization.
3. **Ethical Standards**: Fair labor practices, fair trade partnerships, and community engagement.
4. **Audit Process**: Quarterly third-party audits for transparency.

**Implementation Timeline**:
- Phase 1 (Q3): Draft framework and assign ownership to Sustainability Officer.
- Phase 2 (Q4): Pilot program in plant-research division.
- Phase 3 (Q1): Full rollout across all departments.
