# Initiative 1: Trusted AI Platform for Crop-Specific Phytochemical Optimization

## Objective
Deploy transparent, data-driven AI system guaranteeing consistent potency, safety, and regulatory compliance of medicinal plants.

## Year 3-5 Outcomes
- AI models validated for ≥95% prediction accuracy of active-compound yields
- Open-source provenance ledger for every batch (traceability to seed, climate, inputs)
- Partnership with two national regulatory agencies for fast-track approvals

## Core Capabilities Required
1. **Advanced Machine Learning**
   - Deep phenotyping models
   - Genomics-metabolomics integration

2. **Blockchain Provenance**
   - Immutable audit trails
   - Batch-level traceability

3. **Explainable AI Dashboards**
   - Grower interfaces
   - Regulator transparency tools

## Implementation Phases

### Phase 1 (2025 Q1-Q2): Foundation
- Assemble cross-functional team (AI, Agriculture, Compliance)
- Define phytochemical targets for top 5 medicinal crops
- Establish data pipeline architecture

### Phase 2 (2025 Q3-Q4): Model Development
- Train initial AI models on historical yield data
- Implement blockchain ledger for batch tracking
- Conduct first regulatory consultation

### Phase 3 (2026): Validation
- Deploy in 25 pilot farms
- Validate 95% accuracy threshold
- Submit partnership proposals to regulatory agencies

### Phase 4 (2027-2028): Scale & Compliance
- Expand to 200+ farms
- Achieve regulatory approval
- Open-source provenance system
