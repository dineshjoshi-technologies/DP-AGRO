# Knowledge Base: Medicinal Agriculture & AI Platform

## Version: 1.0

## Purpose
Develop India's most trusted AI-powered medicinal agriculture and technology ecosystem.

## Scope
Ontology, Plants, Products, Manufacturing, Research, Market Intelligence, Exports

## AI Integration
Intelligent knowledge retrieval and decision support for agriculture.

## Version Control
Git-based collaboration with comprehensive documentation tracks.

## Directory Structure
- `templates/` - Reusable templates for knowledge objects
- `knowledgebase/` - Core agricultural knowledge domains
- `ontology/` - Semantic relationships between concepts
- `datasets/` - Structured data for AI training
- `references/` - Source citations and external references
- `assets/` - Images, diagrams, and media
